Ocean Conservation Society was organized to conduct scientific research and educational projects leading to the protection and conservation of our oceans.

We are the only organization conducting long-term, year round research on marine mammals off the coast of Los Angeles, California.

We discovered that dolphins off California suffered skin diseases and physical deformities. It was the FIRST study of its kind! The presence of these diseases is related to man-made pollutants in our waters. .
